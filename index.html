About Me

I am a senior at NYU Tandon School of Engineering studying Mechanical Engineering.
I am passionate about space exploration, whether it be exploring new worlds with rovers
or traveling the vast expanses of space with rockets; basically, anything that contributes
to facing space, humanity’s last great frontier, I want a hand in designing and building.
Additionally, I am captivated by anything that flies; being able to design planes, helicopters,
drones, anything that uses loopholes in the laws of physics to allow humans to defy gravity
and take to the air would be would make me one happy engineer.

To that end, I’ve gained experience in mechanical engineering design through the
design and construction of two rovers entered into the annual NASA Lunabotics
Challenge, as well as a project which prototyped glasses for the visually impaired.
In addition to mechanical engineering skills, I’ve also developed coding skills in
C++ and Python,a knowledge of project management and the systems engineering
process, as well as teamwork, communication, and leadership skills. You can
find more information about these projects below, as well as details for specific
projects in the tabs above.
